Announcement: Semi-Final Month Overview

Hey everyone, this month we are heading into our semi-final period. Our focus will be Unit 3: Display lists and use Material Design. This unit is all about making your apps more beautiful and intuitive with Material Design, animations, and accessibility.

A Note on Personalization (Required)

For all semi-final assignments, you are not allowed to just copy the sample data from the codelabs. The goal is to apply the concepts to your own ideas.

  • For the "Affirmations App": You must provide your own set of affirmations. Using Filipino, Bisaya, or English is highly encouraged.
  • For the "30 Days App": You must create your own, personalized theme. Do not just copy the "Dogs" theme. Examples: "30 Days of Fitness," "30 Cebuano Phrases," or "30 Local Recipes."
